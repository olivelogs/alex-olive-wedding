Our Story

We met in the Colorado State University Plant Diagnostic Clinic in 2023, and now we're getting married. It's really cute.

Life threw trials at both of us. Shortly after Alex moved into a new apartment, there was a gas leak and the hot water was shut off. Olive graciously allowed Alex to stay at their place while maintenance took their sweet time to get things fixed. Eventually the gas leak was sealed and the hot water was turned back on, but Alex never left Olive’s apartment. The rest was history. We learned we lived well together. We worked well together, laughed together, and understood each other. We had a son. His name is Fish. He was, and still is, a trial in his own right.

As we were planning to upgrade our apartment, we had an idea: move across the country. So that’s what we did. And it occurred to us that when someone makes a move across the country easier, not harder, you’ve probably found a keeper. We had more life trials, including a daughter named Bug. Alex got laid off, then started a business. Olive broke their back. The cats got Giardia. And we’ve loved each other through all of it. Our garden grows better together!

We’re really excited to have our wedding at Prairie Fruits Farm & Creamery - when we first visited Champaign-Urbana to scope out apartments, this is where the decision to move here was made. We’ve since visited many times, and are thrilled to have you all there.
